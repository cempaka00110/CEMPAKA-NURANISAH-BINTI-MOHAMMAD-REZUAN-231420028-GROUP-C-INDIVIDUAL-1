ABOUT THE FLORIST

My name is Cempaka Nuranisah binti Mohammad Rezuan, a student from Group C and also carrying the matric number 231420028. Just like the flower I’m named after, I was born to bloom.

From petals to perfume, I find magic in every flower. Their gentle grace, their silent stories, and the way they soften even the hardest of days — all of it speaks to my soul.

It’s no surprise that my dream is to become a florist.

To me, floristry is not just about arranging stems and colors. It's about sharing emotion without words, painting joy with blossoms, and bringing comfort with fragrance and form.

Flowers have always been my quiet companions, my soft inspirations. I long to craft arrangements that whisper beauty, that brighten rooms, that remind people they are loved.
